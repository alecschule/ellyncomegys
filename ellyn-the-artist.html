About Ellyn the Artist

As you can see when looking at Ellyn’s art work, she paints about her spiritual journey in life. Painting has helped her push through obstacles to understand herself better. Ellyn has been painting for nearly 20 years. She has learned to integrate her eye for color and design into her beautiful masterpieces. All of her paintings are oil on canvas.

Ellyn loves to do colorful, adventurous things that include 15-minute adventures with her husband John, rappelling in Colorado, skydiving, and doing mini triathlons. She believes in staying fit and healthy.

Recently Ellyn has been working as an Urgent Care Registered Nurse. Now she is looking forward to working less frequently having time to expand her healing practice.

With Ellyn’s touch, hospital waiting rooms and doctor’s offices will no longer be so sterile looking. Beyond the hospital walls, her artwork has been shown in restaurants, coffee shops, churches, and a healing retreat.
