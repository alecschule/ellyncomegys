About the Healing Process

During the healing session we use conscious connected breathing, also called rebirthing. Conscious connected breathing is deep belly breathing which brings to consciousness and releases old patterns and traumas and allows new patterns to emerge. This process will result in a reduction of stress, detoxing of the body, and increase in metabolism. After a session a client thinks and breathes differently, has more energy, and is more present and available.

During the session we may use any or all of the following: conscious connected breathing, Gestalt reframing, inner child work, Reiki, art therapy, and life coaching techniques. This work is very rewarding and clients can begin opening their heart and lives to new possibilities after one session.

As clients do this healing, they will let of negative experiences and shift their experience of life to become happier, more relaxed, and free to be themselves. This work allows one to be more free, masterful, and whole.

We charge $60 per session which usually lasts about two hours.
